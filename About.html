About Us

Our goal is to improve the quality of life for most people to achieve a better everyday life. A little more joy, more robust and less stress. WellBeing is designed so that it simulates some of the issues and techniques a psychologist would use in his or her first meeting with an individual seeking help.

What drives us

The idea for "WellBeing", came to the team after we witnessed people around go through severe crisis, where a pandemic didn't just ruin our physical but highly affected our mental well being. Through a tough time and needed motivation to change the thought patterns, shift people's focus and set limits, we always wanted to build something, but we were appearing for our boards but now with the exposure and requirements fulfilled and provided to us, we wanted to change the world, maybe just a little bit :)
